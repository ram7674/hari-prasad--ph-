Who is a mentor

“ A mentor empowers a person to see a
possible future, and believe it can be
obtained ”

What is the difference between a teacher and a mentor

“A teacher teaches everybody the same
thing. A mentor helps you discover
yourself”

Who is a good mentor

“Mentorship is about, clear
communication, setting an example,
providing inspiration, stimulating the
thought process, helping set clear goals,
encouraging when chips are down and
promoting ethics ”

Who is a good mentee

“A good mentee is respectful of
mentors time, is respectful of mentors
privacy, is respectful of mentors trust
and keeps following up and checking in
with the mentor”

Benefits of being a good mentee

“A good mentee gets the most of a
mentoring relationship, expands
professional network, gets tools to reach
goals, remains focused and finds new
opportunities”
